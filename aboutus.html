About Us

Hello, we are a non-profit organization that has dedicated all of their free time to reporting and review any album, music video and even outfits that has anything to do with the eastern sensation of kpop. Our passion has led to the creation of this website. Kpop itself is a cultural phenomenon and we even write detailed and comprehensive reviews regarding the newest comebacks from each group. We also discuss the economic conditons of entertainment companies that handle these artists.From discussing Shinee Taemin's new look and reviewing all of BTS's albums to taking a deeper look into NCT Lucas' workout routine and discussing about the new possibilities the girl groups of the new generation. We tend to relate significant cultural and historical facts when discussing kpop, so come and join us in this passionate journey of ours!
